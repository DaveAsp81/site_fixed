## 💰 MONETIZATION SETUP (Critical!)

### Step 1: Google AdSense (Weeks 2-4)

**Why Wait?**
Google AdSense requires 15-20 quality articles and some traffic before approval.

**Application Process:**
1. Go to [google.com/adsense](https://google.com/adsense)
2. Sign up with your site URL
3. Add verification code to your site
4. Wait 1-3 weeks for approval
5. Once approved, place ad codes in marked `[Ad Space]` areas

**Ad Placement (In HTML files):**
```html
<!-- Replace [Ad Space - 728x90] with: -->
<script async src="https://pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js"></script>
<ins class="adsbygoogle"
     style="display:block"
     data-ad-client="ca-pub-XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX"
     data-ad-slot="XXXXXXXXXX"
     data-ad-format="auto"></ins>
<script>
     (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
</script>
```

**Expected Revenue:**
- Month 3-6: $10-50/month
- Month 6-12: $50-200/month
- Year 2+: $200-500+/month (with consistent traffic)
